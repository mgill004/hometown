Top three activities to do in San Ramon

Hike at Las Trampas Regional Park

Check out the Elderberry Ridge Trail for a superb view amongst the rolling hills.

Enjoy dinner and a movie at City Center Bishop Ranch

Stop by the world famous ice cream shop, Salt & Straw, for a sweet treat!

Visit the Bollinger Canyon Fountain for a picture perfect moment

A cascading waterfall and bridge make a beautiful backdrop for your photos.
